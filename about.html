About

This is where I will try to give you a sense of who I am, just who is this person who might be doing your photo session, or photographing your wedding or your children.

I am a photographer based in Pune, India, specializing in portrait, wedding, fashion, children and event photography, but I also photograph landscapes, macro and sports. You can see my latest work on my photography page on Facebook.

I love photography for a variety of reasons. I love to create and appreciate art and I just love science and technology – Photography is the perfect amalgamation of art, science and technology. (By the way – this website is created and maintained by me :) )

It gives me immense happiness to photograph people and seeing a smile break on their faces when I share my work and knowing that I am creating loving and happy memories for them which will last for a lifetime.

Drop me a note if you want me as your photographer, or want more info about my work and photography services.

I would love to hear from you.

- Shantanu Bedarkar
